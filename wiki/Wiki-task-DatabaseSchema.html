Database Schema Overview

profiles
  • id: uuid
  • username: text
  • full_name: text
  • website: text
  • avatar_url: text
  • updated_at: timestamp with time zone
  • email: text
  • phone: text
  • created_at: timestamp with time zone
  • notes: text
  • sort_int: int4
task_headers
  • id: uuid
  • task_header_id: uuid (FK to task_headers.id)
  • student_id: uuid
  • manager_id: uuid
  • assigned_at: timestamp with time zone
  • completed_at: timestamp with time zone
  • abandoned_at: timestamp with time zone
  • step_id: uuid (FK to task_steps.id)
  • sort_int: int4
task_steps
  • id: uuid
  • task_header_id: uuid (FK to task_headers.id)
  • name: text
  • description: text
  • step_order: integer
  • created_at: timestamp with time zone
  • author_id: uuid
  • external_url: text
  • sort_int: int4
task_assignments
  • id: uuid
  • task_header_id: uuid (FK to task_headers.id)
  • student_id: uuid
  • manager_id: uuid
  • assigned_at: timestamp with time zone
  • completed_at: timestamp with time zone
  • abandoned_at: timestamp with time zone
  • step_id: uuid (FK to task_steps.id)
  • sort_int: int4

Comms

  • sort_int: int4
  • CDate: timestamp with time zone
  • CFrom: uuid
  • CTo: uuid
  • CReply: bigint (FK to Comms.Cid)
  • CText: text

app_event_labels

  • sort_int: int4
  • code: text
  • name: text
  • description: text

app_event_log

  • sort_int: int4
  • event_label_id: bigint (FK to app_event_labels.id)
  • actor_id: uuid (FK to profiles.id)
  • affected_id: uuid
  • created_at: timestamp with time zone

role_permissions

  • sort_int: int4
  • role: USER-DEFINED
  • permission: USER-DEFINED

user_roles

  • sort_int: int4
  • user_id: uuid
  • role: USER-DEFINED

Roles and Context

  • Author: Emerges when a profile creates a task.
  • Student: Defined by a task assignment.
  • Manager: Assigned during task creation or via assignment metadata.

Roles are ephemeral and contextual — not inherent to the profile itself.
